Pallet design
software

Wooden packaging makers often produce small batches of custom products. Each article has to be designed first, but most of these companies don't have the resources to create a CAD model of each product since that is a time (and cost) consuming process. Therefore I'm currently working on a FreeCAD based software to automate the design of wooden pallets and crates for a local company. The software currently allows the user to customize an automatically generated 3D model simply by entering the specifications of the pallet/crate. I'm now working to automatically generate the related 2D drawing as well.

RoboCup
robot

You can have a look at our robot in action YouTube. Or dive deeper into the code on GitHub.
RoboCup is a global robotics competition with several specialties. Here you can see the fourth iteration of our robot. I've been responsible for the mechanical design and for part of the coding. I've developed a program which had to find some balls using ML object detection and I've co-developed the line following software.
We developed the robot from scratch and learned a lot along the way. Not only we understood a lot of concepts regarding robotics, we learned to how to organize team-working and how to carry out successfully such a complicated project as well.
For further information you can check out our poster , or you can find us on Twitter.

rotateCam

Developed in 2022 in the middle of what turned out to be the last period of school from home caused by the pandemic. It was a small 3D printed stand for USB webcams controlled with a Telegram bot that allowed us to tilt remotely the stand which was at school. It was created to follow the teachers during lesson. Before that teachers often had to adjust the camera manually to switch between different areas of the blackboard. Luckily the recovery from the pandemic started just before it was finished, so we decided to complete the project anyway, but it has never been actually used apart from some demonstartions.
